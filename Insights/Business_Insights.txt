Business Insights

1. Maharashtra was the highest revenue-generating state with total revenue of ₹12.22 million, followed by Karnataka (₹9.65 million) and Telangana (₹6.29 million).

2. Bengaluru ranked first among all cities with revenue of ₹6.35 million, followed by Hyderabad (₹4.50 million) and Mumbai (₹3.40 million).

3. Set was the best-selling category with 43,033 units sold, while Dupatta was the lowest-selling category with only 3 units sold.

4. Revenue reached its peak in April 2022 and then declined by 8.6% in May and a further 10.7% in June, indicating a downward sales trend after April.

5. B2C customers contributed 99.25% of total revenue (₹78.0 million), while B2B customers accounted for only 0.75%, showing that the business is heavily dependent on the B2C segment.
